Top Supernatural Manhwa


Eclipse of the Shadow Queen

After a rare celestial event, Jin, a humble scholar, discovers that she is the reincarnation of the ancient Shadow Queen. With dark forces awakening, Jin must embrace her past life and learn to control her powers, while navigating treacherous alliances between gods, demons, and humans. Time is running out, and only she can prevent a catastrophe that threatens the realms.

Wings of the Fallen

In a world where angels walk among humans, Hyeon is a fallen angel stripped of her wings and memory. While trying to understand her mysterious past, she teams up with a group of misfits who all have secrets of their own. Together, they uncover a prophecy about an impending war between Heaven and Hell, and only Hyeon can stop it—if she regains her true power.

The Cursed Moon

Every full moon, the quiet village of Gwiyang is haunted by a curse that turns its residents into bloodthirsty creatures. Seol, a fierce young woman, embarks on a journey to break the curse after her younger brother becomes afflicted. Alongside a mysterious stranger who claims to know the origins of the curse, Seol faces supernatural trials that challenge everything she believes.

Phantom Seer

Haru, an ordinary high school student, suddenly develops the ability to see malevolent spirits after a near-death experience. Haunted by terrifying phantoms, she is guided by a mysterious girl who claims to be a phantom seer—a medium able to control spirits. Together, they must confront powerful apparitions and prevent an ancient evil from rising again.

Requiem of the Forgotten

Yumi is a spirit medium who helps lost souls cross over to the afterlife. However, she encounters a strange boy named Kaito who refuses to move on. As they uncover the dark history behind Kaito’s death, they discover a plot that binds the living and the dead together in a curse that threatens to collapse both realms.

Sorceress of the Abyss

Hye-rin, a young sorceress, is chosen to become the next wielder of the Abyss, a dark force that grants unimaginable power but devours the soul of its user. While training to control the Abyss, she must fend off enemies who seek the power for themselves. Along her journey, Hye-rin must choose between saving her world or surrendering to the darkness within.
